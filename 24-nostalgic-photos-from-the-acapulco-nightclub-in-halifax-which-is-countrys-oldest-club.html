24 nostalgic photos from the Acapulco nightclub in Halifax which is country's oldest club

June 26, 2024

The ‘Acca’ opened in 1961 and has been visited by many VIPs from singers to actors

A West Yorkshire club said to be the oldest in the country has marked its 60th birthday.

Acapulco in Halifax – affectionately known as the Acca – opened in 1961 and is reputed to be the oldest club to have stuck with the original name.

It has been entertaining people since before America put a man on the Moon.

For more Halifax news, click here.

The ‘Acca’ was one of the first clubs in the world to have its own web page which was live during the 1990s.

The club on Waterhouse Street has hosted many famous faces over the years including Michaela Strachan from the Hitman and Her TV show, singer Sinitta and a number of soap and TV stars.

The Acapulco is hosting a vaccination clinic on Sunday October 31 that is being billed by the club as “the UK’s first halloween-themed vaccination clinic.”

The event, backed by the NHS, will run from 1pm to 4pm, and is for people who need their first or second jab.

Everyone that receives a vaccine at the clinic will receive a SubWay voucher, and an Acca pass that will entitle you to three free entries, Valid till November 30, 2021.
