Days of Our Lives Spoilers – Nicole Walker Hopes The Baby Is Eric Brady’s

Days Of Our Lives spoilers reveal that Nicole wants the baby to be Eric’s. However, Nicole isn’t willing to risk telling Eric about the child because she doesn’t want to hurt him. Nicole plans to get the paternity test done before letting either man know about the pregnancy so she knows who the father is. Unfortunately, things may not go her way.

Two can keep a secret if one is dead and now four people know this one. Sloan Petersen (Jessica Serfaty) is the latest one to find out about Nicole’s pregnancy and has promised to keep quiet. Kayla Johnson (Mary Beth Evans) is legally bound to stay quiet about it. Rumors suggest that Anna Devane (Leanne Hunley) will let it slip that Nicole is pregnant.

Days of Our Lives Updates – EJ Dimera Proposes To Nicole Walker

EJ will abruptly propose to Nicole but it is unclear if this is before or after he learns about the baby. EJ wants to hold onto Nicole so he isn’t beyond having the paternity test rigged. EJ will automatically assume the baby is his when he learns about the child but he may not let Nicole know that he is aware of her pregnancy. After all, he doesn’t want her to think he is marrying her for the baby.

Nicole is not going to be willing to say yes to EJ until she has this paternity test so she may be forced to admit that she’s pregnant when he pushes the issue. EJ is very possessive over his children and certainly won’t want Nicole tied to Eric in any way. Since Nicole and Eric are supposedly over, look for EJ to make sure the test says the baby is his.

Eric Brady Is Cheated Out Of Another Child

Eric may not know that Nicole is pregnant but this is just one more opportunity for him to be a father. Eric has always wanted a child and he would certainly love to have one with Nicole. This baby could lead to the reunion that both Eric and Nicole are hoping for if it turns out to be his. However, Eric won’t question the paternity test results if Kayla Johnson (Mary Beth Evans) agrees to supervise.

Eric may never know that there was even the possibility that this baby was his. Nicole was using birth control at the time. Nicole never told Eric about the baby. Why would he even consider the prospect? Unless something comes up with this baby’s health, Eric would never need to know the truth if the test is changed. Will EJ go so far as to steal Eric’s baby?
