What is John David Washington’s Net Worth and Salary?

An ex-professional footballer turned American actor, John David Washington, holds a net worth of $8 million.

He rose to prominence portraying undercover detective Ron Stallworth in Spike Lee’s 2018 film “BlacKkKlansman.”

His contributions extend to the HBO series “Ballers” and movies like “Tenet,” “Malcolm & Marie,” “Beckett,” and “Canterbury Glass.”

Prior to his acting career, Washington spent four years playing football for the UFL’s Sacramento Mountain Lions.

Early Life

Born on July 28, 1984, in Los Angeles, California, John David Washington is the eldest among actor Denzel Washington and actress-singer Pauletta Washington’s four children.

His early exposure to the film industry came through appearances in movies, debuting as a seven-year-old student in Spike Lee’s 1992 film “Malcolm X,” starring his father.

During his teenage years, Washington attended Campbell High School, excelling in basketball, football, and track.

He graduated in 2002 and pursued higher education at Morehouse College in Atlanta, Georgia

Film Career

As an adult, Washington made his debut film appearance in the 2017 musical drama “Love Beats Rhymes,” directed by rapper RZA.

The film features Azealia Banks, Common, Lucien Laviscount, Jill Scott, and Lorraine Toussaint. However, 2018 marked a turning point in his film career, appearing in four films.

“Monsters and Men” and “Monster” premiered at the Sundance Film Festival, with Washington starring as police officer Dennis Williams in the former and playing the supporting role of Richard “Bobo” Evans in the latter.

His standout performance came in Spike Lee’s “BlacKkKlansman,” portraying real-life detective Ron Stallworth infiltrating a local Ku Klux Klan chapter, earning critical acclaim and SAG and Golden Globe Award nominations.

He rounded off 2018 with “The Old Man & the Gun.”

Impressed by his work in “BlacKkKlansman,” Christopher Nolan cast Washington in “Tenet,” a 2020 science-fiction spy film alongside Robert Pattinson, Elizabeth Debicki, Michael Caine, and Kenneth Branagh.

He later starred opposite Zendaya in the 2021 romantic drama “Malcolm & Marie,” filmed entirely during the COVID-19 pandemic.

In 2021, he appeared in the action thriller “Beckett,” portraying a hunted American tourist in Greece.

Additionally, his credits include David O. Russell’s “Canterbury Glass,” featuring Christian Bale, Margot Robbie, Robert De Niro, and Rami Malek, and Gareth Edwards’ “True Love,” with Gemma Chan, Ken Watanabe, and Allison Janney among the cast.

John David Washington – Achievements

Washington was honored with the Saturn Award for Best Actor for his role in the drama film “Malcolm & Marie,” co-starring with Zendaya in the lead roles (2021).

Additionally, he received IMDb STARmeter Awards as the Breakout Star in 2018.

Moreover, he has received numerous nominations for the People’s Choice Award over the years.
