Once again, this highlight is proof that WrestleMania is home of some of the biggest moments in wrestling history.

In 2001, arguably the match of the night, was the Tables, Ladders and Chairs triple threat match for the Tag Team Championships with Edge and Christian, the Dudley Boys and the Hardy Boys. And while there were plenty of extreme moments, this was by far the best.

Jeff Hardy made the attempt to grab the belts but was left dangling above the ring after Bubba Ray Dudley removed the ladder. Edge, on top of another ladder in the corner, jumped to spear the hanging Hardy in mid air.
