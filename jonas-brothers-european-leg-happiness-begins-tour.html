Jonas Brothers announce European leg of Happiness Begins Tour

May 16, 2024
blog

Jonas Brothers have delighted their European fans with the news that they will be bringing their Happiness Begins Tour to the UK and Europe in 2020.

Kicking off in Birmingham on January 29, Joe, Kevin and Nick will be playing 15 dates including a show at London’s O2 Arena. Other cities they’ll be performing at include Manchester, Berlin, Milan, Madrid, Barcelona, Amsterdam and Paris.

The tour is in support of their forthcoming album Happiness Begins, which is released on June 7. The album includes ‘Sucker’ and ‘Cool’, and has been executive produced by OneRepublic’s Ryan Tedder.

Tickets for the tour go on general sale on June 6, and UK fans can access a pre-sale by pre-ordering the album here.

The tour announcement follows a promotional two week trip across Europe, which included a sold out show at Pryzm, Kingston and an appearance on The Graham Norton Show, which airs tonight.
