Elley Duhé Biography

Elley Duhé is an American singer, songwriter, and television personality best known as a featured artist on a Happy Now single made in collaboration with Russian-German music producer Zedd and released in July 2018. The song reached #90 on the US Billboard Hot 100 chart and was also awarded a RIAA gold “Gold” star. She started her career as a solo singer and songwriter with her debut single Millennium, produced with music producer Tarro, in October 2016. A large audience noticed the single and received more than 2 million views on YouTube and shared on Spotify over 1.5 million times. Her second single, titled Immortal debuted through Billboard in December of the same year, reached 4.5 million Spotify streams and was also remixed by two British DJs known as Snakehips.
