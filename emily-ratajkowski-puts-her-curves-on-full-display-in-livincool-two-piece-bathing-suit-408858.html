Emily Ratajkowski Puts Her Curves On Full Display In Livincool Two-Piece Bathing Suit

June 21, 2024
blog

Emily Ratajkowski is putting her curves on full display in several Livincool throwback photos. Since Emily started her own clothing line Inamorata Woman, she mostly poses in her own garments — including her bikinis and one-piece bathing suits. Things took a bit of an unusual turn when Emily shared a photo of herself seated on an airplane with her 26.6 million Instagram followers and thanked the clothing company Livincool for the photo. It appears that Livincool sent Emily a framed print of herself from the flight. Emily shared the original photo and then a second picture of the framed print on her wall. Her dog Colombo could be seen in the photo’s background looking on from the kitchen.

Emily hashtagged Livincool in the Instagram post and at the same time as Emily’s post, Livincool shared several throwback photos of Emily looking stunning in their swimwear.

Livincool shared the following photo slideshow with the caption that you may see below.

“Summertime at its best @emrata #LivincoolArchives.”

Emily looked gorgeous with a picturesque setting that included blue, tranquil water in the background. Emily wore her hair parted down the middle and captured in a low twist at the nape of her neck. Holding a glass of wine in one hand and an oyster on a shell in the other, Emily gave the camera a sultry look.

Her makeup was natural in appearance and looked sun-kissed with coral lipstick and eyeshadow. Emily flaunted her gorgeous figure and showed off her ample cleavage and toned abs in the pose.

A second photo in the slideshow captured Emily in the same setting, only instead of looking to the side, she stared directly into the camera. A third photo showed Emily’s backside as she walked upon rocks in only a thong.
