Who is Brenda Tracy?

Brenda Tracy is a survivor of sexual assault and an activist tirelessly dedicated to ending sexual violence and transforming the culture surrounding college sports.

Though she reported the assault to the police, the case was never prosecuted.

Brenda kept her traumatic experience private for years until she courageously shared it with a columnist in 2014.

Today, she works relentlessly to combat sexual misconduct on college campuses and within athletic communities through her non-profit organization, Set The Expectation.

Brenda also speaks openly about her journey and advocates for legislation that supports the rights of victims.
