YEEZY’S BACK

Kanye West returned to Instagram wearing a black face mask and a necklace bearing his children’s names.

The rapper – who recently compared his home with Kim Kardashian to prison – cropped up on social media in a series of moody shots to promote his new album Donda.

Kanye shared close-ups of his gold chain necklace, which had the names of his four kids with Kim – North, eight, Saint, five, Chicago, three, and Psalm, two.

Saint was seen wearing a similar necklace with his name on it in a photo Kim posted on Instagram this week, captioned: “Just how?”

In other photos from the shoot, Kanye wore leather pants, a black hoodie, gloves and a black mask that covered his entire head as well as goggles, while crouching on top of two silver drums.
