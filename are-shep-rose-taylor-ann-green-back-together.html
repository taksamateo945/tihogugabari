Are Shep Rose &amp; Taylor Ann Green Back Together?

March 13, 2024
blog

The breakup of Shep Rose and Taylor Ann Green was less than shocking to many fans of Southern Charm, who witnessed the ups and downs of their rocky relationship. On the season 7 reunion, Shep even admitted to kissing another woman in a stairwell.

Their recent split was reportedly instigated by Taylor, who (in the current season) has been vocal about her trust issues, to Shep’s chagrin. Though the relationship seemed over, Shep posted an August 17 photo of himself with his ex, who seemed to be wearing a swimsuit. Many viewers believe the relationship isn’t quite over.

According to fan account Queens of Bravo, a viewer just spotted Shep and Taylor at a Charleston restaurant called Barsa Tapas Lounge & Bar. The source claimed their behavior was “super loved up.”

In a photo that was taken at the restaurant, Taylor and Shep are hanging out, with drinks.

Is this a possible sign of reconciliation? Or were they never truly broken up? Castmate Olivia Flowers initially believed the split was temporary. As of now, Shep and Taylor have not publicly confirmed this.

Many viewers accused Shep of stringing Taylor along, and believe he has no real intention of marrying her or having children.

On a recent episode of the show, the 42-year-old yelled at Taylor, calling her a “f**king idiot” during an egg-toss game. After the incident, Naomie Olindo called his behavior “abusive.”

Southern Charm season eight airs Thursdays at 9/8c on Bravo.
