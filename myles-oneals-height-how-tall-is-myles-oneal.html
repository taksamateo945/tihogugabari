He Has The Ideal Height for a Male Model

Myles O’Neal’s height of 6 feet 2 inches places him in the perfect zone for the career that he has chosen for himself. A simple online search will reveal that the high fashion modeling industry has strict standards for models, both male and female. Male models are required to be between 5 feet 11 inches and 6 feet 3 inches. Anything below or above will be considered either too short or too tall, respectively, and the individual involved may find it hard to land runway work.

They may be able to land other types of modeling work, though. Luckily for Myles O’Neal, he is of the ideal height, and as such, his career is not restricted.
