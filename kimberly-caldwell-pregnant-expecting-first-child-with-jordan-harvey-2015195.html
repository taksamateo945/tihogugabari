Kimberly Caldwell Pregnant, Expecting First Child With Jordan Harvey

May 18, 2024
blog

Baby bumpin’! Kimberly Caldwell is pregnant and expecting her first child with husband Jordan Harvey, her rep confirms to Us Weekly. The American Idol alum took to Instagram to announce the happy news herself, even revealing she’s picked out a name for her future daughter.

“So thrilled to finally announce our baby girl to the world at 12 weeks along! @jordancharvey #firsttimesacharm #HappyEaster,” the songstress shared on Instagram two months ago, before revealing her soon-to-be mini-me’s name with a sonogram video on April 26.

“Meet Harlow Monroe Harvey. Had another chance to get a glimpse of our girl at almost 15 weeks. #singingbaby #BabyHarlow @jordancharvey,” she captioned the clip. 

This will be the first child for the couple, who said “I do” this past December in Palm Springs, Calif.

“First day to rock my bump! #herewego #babyharlow #16weeks,” the mom-to-be captioned a mirror selfie of herself proudly showing off her bump on May 4.
